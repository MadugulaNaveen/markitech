About Us

Markitech Digital is a full-service digital marketing agency founded back in 2019 that is dedicated to providing industry-leading marketing solutions to clients in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia and beyond. We take pride in equipping digital marketers to become experts in their field, providing them with the tools and necessary resources to stay ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving digital landscape. As an agency that specializes in digital marketing, we fully understand the importance of a comprehensive approach to effectively promote our clients' businesses online.
